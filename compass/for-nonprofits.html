Add a live world-problem card to your site. Free.

One line of HTML puts a clear, honest card for any of twenty-five world problems on your page: the real scale, the trend, and a link to what actually works against it. It updates itself, costs nothing, needs no account, and quietly points your readers toward understanding and action.

🤔 Why put this on your site
It's genuinely free
No account, no cost, no tracking cookies dropped on your visitors, no strings. Built as a public good.
It stays honest and current
Figures are approximations drawn from public sources like Our World in Data, WHO, UNICEF, and GiveWell-style analysis. The card updates without you touching the code.
It helps your readers act
Each card links to a deeper page: root causes, what the evidence says works, honest cost-per-outcome, and concrete ways to help with money, time, skills, or voice.
It's light and safe
A single sandboxed iframe. It loads lazily, won't slow your page, and can't touch the rest of your site.
