The long view

200 years of human progress, in the numbers

These are the trajectories Hans Rosling built Factfulness around — the changes so slow and so vast the news never shows them. Extreme poverty, child mortality and illiteracy have collapsed; one metric, CO₂, is the honest counterweight that got worse. Figures are well-established historical estimates, approximate by design, and anything past today is projection, not data. Open the interactive 1800→2100 slider →
